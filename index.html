AI for SE & Cybersecurity

I am an R&T Scientist at the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST). My research focuses on harnessing Artificial Intelligence to automate processes and enhance practices in software engineering and cybersecurity. Before this role, I was a Research Associate at the University of Luxembourg, in the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT), where I was a member of the Trustworthy Software Engineering (TruX) Research Group. I completed my Ph.D. at the University of Luxembourg under the guidance of Prof. Jacques Klein, Prof. Tegawendé F. Bissyandé, and external supervisor Prof. Lorenzo Cavallaro, during which I served as a Doctoral Researcher.
